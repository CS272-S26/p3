The Pistols at Dawn

Surf Rock from Madison, WI

The Pistols at Dawn are a Madison instrumental surf band shaped by punk energy, metal intensity, vintage reverb, and a deep connection to the local DIY scene.

Formed in a basement on Baldwin Street and tied closely to the Crystal Corner and Madison punk community, the band has spent the last two decades building a loud, strange, and unmistakable sound.

Out Now

The Halfway House Tapes (Smart Studios, 2007) is now streaming. These long-unreleased recordings capture the band in its early live form and mark the 20th anniversary of The Pistols at Dawn.
